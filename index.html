Onboarding Steps

Complete these essential steps to set up your account and unlock candidate verification.

Setup Action items

Join the Microsoft Student Developer Community on Discord Completed

You have joined the Discord server! This is the primary community hub for notifications, questions, and networking.

Link Discord Account to GitHub in `#start-here`

Go to the #start-here channel on the Discord server. Locate the verification bot's message. Follow the interactive steps to link your Discord account with your GitHub account. This unlocks the rest of the server channels (like #registration-help, #announcements, etc.).

Link Azure for Students & Verify Academic Status

Confirm your student status in the Student Ambassadors Portal. You must verify and sign up for an Azure for Students account (requires your academic email or school verification). Ensure you use the same Microsoft Account email you registered with.

Complete the Cloud Skills Challenge Technical Training

As a candidate, you are required to complete a selected Microsoft Learn Learning Path (Technical Training) to demonstrate commitment. Check the Discord server's #announcements or #start-here for the active challenge link. Remember to use a link containing your Contributor ID to access it!

Complete and Log Your First Registration Activity

Share a useful Microsoft Learn course on your LinkedIn, Twitter, or Discord community. Make sure to generate the contributor-tagged URL in the URL Builder first! Once done, log the activity in the Activity Log tab.

Pro-Tips for Registered Members

1
Keep Emails Aligned

Make sure you use the exact same Microsoft account email address for: the MLSA portal, Microsoft Learn, Azure for Students, and your Discord/GitHub linkage. If the emails differ, your progress tracking will fail.

2
Track Everything

Whenever you share a post, host a small chat, or guide someone, take a quick screenshot and log it in the Activity tab. Having detailed logs will save you tons of time when submitting milestone reviews.

3
Stay Consistent

Completing onboarding is the first hurdle. Try to share at least 1-2 tagged items per week and check the Discord server for technical challenges to fast-track your approval.
